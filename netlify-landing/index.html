The problem it solves

Writing Arabic should not depend on having the right keyboard layout, or slowing down to hunt for Arabic letters.

⌨️

No Arabic keyboard?

You want to write in Fusha or Tunisian dialect but only have a Latin keyboard. Type naturally in Roman letters and get correct Arabic script in seconds.

⚡

Prefer Latin / Arabizi?

You can use an Arabic keyboard, but Arabizi is faster for you: 3aslema, 7alek, 9rib. Convert to polished Arabic without retyping every word.

Messages, posts, emails, notes. Open the extension popup, paste or type, copy the result, and keep going. No tab switching or separate app required.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about Arabizzi, privacy, and how conversion works.

What is Arabizzi?

A free Chrome extension that converts Arabizi and Latin text into colloquial Arabic script or Modern Standard Arabic (Fusha), powered by Google Gemini.

Is it free?

Yes. Arabizzi is free to install. You use your own Gemini API key from Google AI Studio, which includes a free tier.

Do I need an Arabic keyboard?

No. Type in Latin letters — like 3aslema or chneya 7alek — and copy the Arabic output anywhere.

How is my data handled?

Your API key and settings stay in your browser. Text is sent only to Google Gemini when you convert. Read our privacy policy.

Open for contributions and new ideas

Arabizzi is an open project. We welcome bug reports, feature suggestions, translations, and pull requests from the community.
