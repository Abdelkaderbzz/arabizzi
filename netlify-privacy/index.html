Privacy Policy — Arabic Converter

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Arabic Converter is a Chrome extension that converts Tunisian Arabic written in Latin letters into Arabic script or Modern Standard Arabic (Fusha).

Information we handle

1. Text you enter for conversion

When you click Convert, the text you type is sent to Google's Generative Language API (Gemini) to produce the converted Arabic text. This is required for the extension to work.

2. Your Gemini API key

You provide your own Google Gemini API key. It is stored only in your browser using Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local). We do not receive or store your API key on any server operated by us.

3. Local preferences and history

Language preference, output mode (Fusha/Tunisian), and recent conversion history (up to 10 entries) are stored locally in your browser. This data is not sent to us.

How we use data

Data is used solely to convert text you submit and to remember your settings and recent conversions in your browser.

Third parties

Conversion text is transmitted to Google (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) using your API key, under Google's terms and privacy policy. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or other third parties.

Data retention

Local data remains in your browser until you clear extension storage or uninstall the extension.

Children

This extension is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above will reflect changes.
